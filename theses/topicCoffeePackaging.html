AI-Based Recognition of Coffee Bean Packaging for Automatic Brewing Profile Retrieval

Context: This bachelor thesis will be conducted with datum.coffee, a hardware and software startup in Innsbruck developing a coffee machine with grinder that combines intuitive operation with barista-level espresso quality, complemented by an open platform and app for automatically finding optimal brew and grind parameters per coffee bean.

Goal: Research, design, and prototype a camera-based AI solution that recognizes coffee bean packages, extracts relevant packaging information, and matches the extracted data against a database to identify the coffee and retrieve corresponding brewing and grinding profiles.

Work Packages:

  • Package Detection & Classification: Build a computer vision pipeline for robust package recognition under real-world conditions.
  • Information Extraction: Parse labels for brand, product name, roast level, origin, processing, roast date, and barcode.
  • Database Matching: Implement confidence scoring and fallback strategies for incomplete or ambiguous data.
  • Evaluation: Validate recognition accuracy, extraction quality, and runtime suitability with varying lighting, angles, reflections, and occlusions.
