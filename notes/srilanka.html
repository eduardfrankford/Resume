My top 5 things to do in Sri Lanka

Posted on 14th of March, 2022 by Eduard Frankford
Sri Lanka Travel Safari Surf

1. Spent some days at the Ocean View Hotel in Bentota

The Ocean View hotel in Bentota is the perfect place to start your Sri Lanka experience, because it is not far away from Columbo. It can be reached by train for less than 1 Euro or if you want to travel comfortably, you can take a taxi for around 40 Euros. The manager there was the most courteous guy we met during the whole trip. He gave us many valuable tips and showed us many cool things. Generally, Bentota is a rather calm place in Sri lanka and it has a nice river with mangroves, where you can do boat trips. During a boat trip we saw many warans, monkeys and crocodiles. Furthermore, in Bentota you can visit a turtle hatchery, where you can learn about why turtles are endangered in Sri Lanka and you can even see an albino turtle.

2. Go surf in Weligama area

Weligama is the Surf-mekka in Sri Lanka. You can see tuk-tuks with surfboards on the roof everywhere. However, as the town is so famous for its nice beginner waves I would recommend to you to go surf at Coconut Beach which is 10 minutes by tuck tuck in the direction to Midigama and should cost no more than 3 Euros if you start in Weligama to get there. Coconut Beach is a nice reef break. There you can find two beach bars which sell Lion Beer and Coconuts. Furthermore you can rent surfboards there for a cheap price. I paid 1.5 Euros per hour. Also if you want to go to a beach party ask the locals if they are planning something for the days you are there. Normally, they organize at least one party a week.

3. Book a whale watching tour in Mirissa

Mirissa is the whale watching capital of Sri Lanka. There you can easily find somebody by whom you can book a day trip by boat to go search for these beautiful animals. However, be careful about the price, because the prices can range from 25 Euros to 50 Euros per person. So don't forget to negotiate.

4. Visit SK Town near Dondra Beach

The place called "Dondra bay", was the best place of our whole journey. There we booked athe beautiful hotel called The Seascape, which is located directly at the beach. Another big advantage is that you can directly see the line up when you open your curtains in the morning. If you need a surf lesson I can warmly recommend to you Surf Sk-town which is a surf school owned by a local whose mom has a beach restaurant where you can eat the best curry and the cheapest and best prawns I have eaten in Sri Lanka.

5. Visit Yala National Park in the southeast of Sri Lanka

Yala national park is located in the southeast of Sri Lanka. As all the safaris start very early in the morning I recommend to you to stay at least 2 nights there. My girlfriend and I stayed at a calm place called Tara Watergate which is located directly at a lake. On the safari we saw elephants, crocodiles, leppards, monkeys, many birds, warans and countless other animals. I was really impressed with the variety that nature holds in this small country. We stayed in the park the whole day and when we got home 12 hours after we left, we were pretty tired and overloaded with impressions. A very memorable day I have to say. The next day we went back to Dondra bay, because we loved it there so much that we booked 3 more nights in the very same hotel that we have been to before. And once again we were not disappointed and enjoyed every minute there.
