:: About Me :: Hello! I am currently a Doctoral Student and Senior Scientist in Computer Science at the University of Innsbruck, building on my soon-to-be-finished Master in Software Engineering and Master's in International Economics. I am multilingual, proficient in Spanish, French, English, and German, a skill that has enriched my global collaborations and engagements. In addition to my academic pursuits, I recently founded Frankford's IT-Solutions. As a dynamic startup, we specialize in the development of custom software, website design, and process automation solutions tailored to our client's unique business needs. My journey has been marked by discipline and resilience, honed during my years as a member of the Taekwondo National Team of Austria. This experience has taken me to various international tournaments and training camps in Iran, China, Taiwan, Russia, and beyond. My practical experience is further complemented by my role as a software developer for the CodeAbility project at the University of Innsbruck. Beyond academics and business, I am deeply passionate about promoting physical fitness and discipline. As such, I volunteer my time as a coach at my sports club, Frankford-Taekwondo. This role allows me to give back to the community by nurturing the next generation of Taekwondo athletes. To explore more about my professional journey, research, and global adventures, please visit my personal website: www.eduardfrankford.com or www.frankford-it.at.
